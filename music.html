Music

Being a self-taught musician/producer may be one of my greatest accomplishments. Music has always been a huge passion of mine as well. I was around the age of 12 when I got my first drum set, and i've still got it. I wouldn't trade it for the world. Over the years, I have played in multiple bands, though I soon came to realized that I thorougly enjoyed composing everything myself. I had more control over the sound I wanted. That's about the time I found the DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) Ableton. I slowly started "youtubing" everything about the program, and it all just clicked after a while. Too be completely honest, the compositions I began with, looking back on them now, are just down right cringy. You have to start from somewhere though. So fast forward to present day, i'm still making music on the side, i'm just not nearly as active as I once was. Below I have posted my soundcloud player and some YouTube videos which has most of the music i've written, definitely not all of it. I'm almost positive i've got well over 200+ unfinished "ideas" on my hard drive.
